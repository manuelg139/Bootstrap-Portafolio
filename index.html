Manuel Frayre is a Mexican-born Photographer and Creative Director based in Texas. Early in his career he produced and directed music festivals in North Texas where he found a passion for social media event marketing and event photography. He has gone to photograph major festivals like SXSW, Astrowrold, Rolling Loud, JMBLYA and many national tours. He went on to become the editor for indie Instagram aesthetics magazine Nakid Mag, where he developed a deeper understanding of experimental editorial techniques and different styles of photography. His work often highlights LGBTQ+ artists, pridefully showcasing the queer community nationwide with a streetstyle 90's high fashion vibe. His work features acts like Tay Money, Adore Delano and JMSN.
